Intention - I want to write stories that gently tug at your senses like sipping from a cup of blooming tea flowers.

I want to lead you, the literary explorer, into paradoxical worlds where parallels collide and constructs of time warp amongst swirlings of picturesque expressions. Where unorthodoxy and perfectly imagined circumstances mingle, becoming two friends or lovers on a rainy day in the park with red umbrellas.

Take my hand. Let us frolic in the land of words and symbols and breath. Let us embrace what bubbles up in the deepest points and shallowest peaks of our human existence.
